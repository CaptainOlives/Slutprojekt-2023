Travel with us!

The Olive Tree Tourism program takes visitors on a journey through the beautiful landscapes of Greece, where the iconic olive tree has been cultivated for thousands of years. Guests will have the chance to visit olive groves and witness the olive harvest, as well as participate in olive oil production and tastings. They will also have the opportunity to explore the cultural and historical significance of olives, visiting ancient sites and museums that showcase the importance of olives in Greece's past and present.

Whether you're a food lover, history buff, or simply seeking a unique travel experience, Olivers Olives Inc. offers a truly one-of-a-kind adventure that celebrates the greatness of olives and the richness of Greek culture. Contact us today to learn more about our Olive Tree Tourism program.
