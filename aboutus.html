Oliver Olives Inc. is a company that specializes in olive tourism, offering unique and immersive experiences that allow visitors to explore the beauty and history of Greek olives. Our tours are designed to provide an authentic and unforgettable experience, showcasing the rich cultural heritage and health benefits associated with olives.
Our Olive Tree Tourism program offers a range of tours and activities that cater to all interests and experience levels. Whether you're a food lover, a history buff, or simply seeking a relaxing getaway, our team of expert guides and local partners will help you create a personalized itinerary that meets your needs and preferences.

We love olives!

We are excited to share with you the upcoming International Olives Day, a special celebration dedicated to the beloved fruit that we hold dear to our hearts. This annual event, observed on 1st of June, is a global tribute to olives and an opportunity to recognize their significance in various cultures around the world.
International Olives Day is a time to appreciate the rich history, cultural heritage, and health benefits associated with olives. It serves as a reminder of the countless ways in which olives have enriched our lives, from their culinary versatility to their positive impact on our well-being.
At Olivers Olives, we are passionate about promoting the greatness of olives, and International Olives Day holds a special place in our hearts. It allows us to further celebrate the fruit that has been a source of inspiration for our company and to engage with olive enthusiasts like you in a shared celebration.
To mark this occasion, we have prepared special activities and promotions for our valued customers. Be sure to visit our website on International Olives Day to discover exclusive content, exciting offers, and the latest updates from the world of olives.
We encourage you to join us in celebrating International Olives Day by sharing your love for olives with friends, family, and social media followers. Use the hashtag #InternationalOlivesDay to connect with the global olive community and spread the joy and appreciation for this remarkable fruit.
